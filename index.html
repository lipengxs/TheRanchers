About The Ranchers

The Ranchers is an open world country-life simulation game developed by RedPilz Studio and published by Trophy Games. The Ranchers has captured the attention of over 350,000 players on Steam wishlists, making it one of the most anticipated farming simulation games. The Ranchers combines the peaceful tranquility of farm life with the excitement of exploration and cooperative multiplayer gameplay. Set in a vast open world countryside, The Ranchers places players in the role of a new rancher who has left city life behind to start a new adventure. The core themes of The Ranchers explore the balance between work and leisure, the satisfaction of building something from nothing, and the joy of sharing your ranch with friends. The Ranchers features beautiful rural landscapes, diverse animal husbandry, and modern farming mechanics that bring the country life to life.

Game Setting & World

In The Ranchers, players explore a gigantic open world filled with opportunities and challenges. The game world in The Ranchers includes vast farmlands, wild islands, mines, shipwrecks, and dangerous dungeons where monsters guard rare resources. Players in The Ranchers can travel on foot, horseback, scooter, pickup truck, or even yacht to explore every corner of the world. The world of The Ranchers is not just about farming, but about adventure, discovery, and building your dream ranch. From clearing land and planting crops to raising animals and constructing buildings, The Ranchers offers endless possibilities for creative ranch management.

Core Gameplay Features

The Ranchers features comprehensive farming and ranching systems that allow players to grow crops, raise animals, and manage their ranch efficiently. Players in The Ranchers can raise both mammal animals and oviparous animals, each requiring different care and housing. The auction market in The Ranchers allows players to sell resources to other players, maximizing profits through strategic trading. Modern features in The Ranchers include energy systems with wind turbines and solar panels, allowing players to generate and sell electricity to the village.
