英文评论
The Ranchers fans, check out the-ranchers.com if you haven’t already. It’s a solid info hub for the game: guides, screenshots, wiki (animals, buildings, vehicles, items), and a clear countdown to the March 20, 2026 launch. The guides cover farming, ranching, building, and exploration, and the media gallery has plenty of in-game screenshots. It’s a good place to prep before the Steam Next Fest demo.
